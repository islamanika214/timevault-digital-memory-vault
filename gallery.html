Sands of Time

Digital Legacy

Time capsules have been used for centuries to preserve artifacts, letters, and cultural memorabilia for future generations. Some of the most famous examples include the 1939 Westinghouse Time Capsule, buried at the New York World's Fair, and the Crypt of Civilization, sealed in 1940 with thousands of historical items.

These capsules provide a fascinating glimpse into past societies, their values, and their hopes for the future. As technology evolves, digital time capsules are becoming more prevalent, ensuring that memories and messages are preserved in innovative ways.
